About Dhillika Foundation

The Trust is dedicated to the welfare and upliftment of underprivileged communities by providing support for their basic needs, improving their living conditions, and helping them access opportunities for growth. It strives to promote and protect human rights by spreading awareness, conducting outreach programs, and assisting individuals who face injustice or discrimination. A strong focus is placed on youth empowerment through initiatives that build confidence, develop practical and leadership skills, and guide young people toward productive careers. The Trust also works to improve education by establishing and supporting schools, training institutes, and literacy programs to ensure that quality learning is accessible to all. Furthermore, it aims to generate employment by offering skill-development workshops, vocational training, and resources that encourage self-employment and entrepreneurship. Along with these primary goals, the Trust may also undertake additional activities that naturally support, complement, or enhance the fulfillment of its core charitable objectives.
